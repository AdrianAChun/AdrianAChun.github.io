How To Play


You probably already know how to play Tic-Tac-Toe. It's a really simple game, right? That's what most people think. But if you really wrap your brain around it, you'll discover that Tic-Tac-Toe isn't quite as simple as you think!


Step 1: Start the program and it will ask you for the username



Step 2: Type your username and then the game menu will show up

Type " 1 " and " Enter " to enter a game with computer

Type " 2 " and " Enter " to enter a game with a friend

Type " 3 " and " Enter " to check your how many games you played!

Type " 4 " and " Enter " to exit the game



Step 3: Select your side (X or O) and choose your first spot

Tips: When you choose a spot, just simply type the spot number :)



Step 4: Wait for the computer or your friend to choose the spot

Play with Computer

Play with friend

If you win the game, ***Congratulation*** You are the TicTacToe's Master

If you lose the game, ***No Worries*** Practice makes prefect

If you tie the game, ***Take a Deep breath*** The game will restart automaticlly and Time to fight again
